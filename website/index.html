About Us

Our Journey

Janajyoti Vidyamandir was established in 2001 with a vision to provide quality education to the community. Over the years, we have grown into a premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and character building.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To nurture young minds through holistic education, fostering intellectual curiosity, ethical values, and social responsibility.

Vision: To be a leading institution that empowers students to become innovative leaders and compassionate global citizens.

Principal's Welcome

Dear Parents and Students,

Welcome to Janajyoti Vidyamandir! As principal, I am honored to lead an institution where every child is valued and encouraged to reach their full potential. Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and nurturing environment create the perfect foundation for lifelong learning.

Together, we will continue to uphold our tradition of excellence while embracing innovation in education.

- Mr. Rajesh Kumar, Principal

Admission

Admission Open for 2026

Applications are now open for Nursery to Grade 12. Limited seats available!

Admission Process

  1. Inquiry: Visit our campus or website to learn about our programs
  2. Form Submission: Complete and submit the application form
  3. Document Verification: Submit required documents for verification
  4. Confirmation: Receive admission confirmation and fee details

Online Application Form

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery: Minimum age 3+ years as of June 1st
  • LKG: Minimum age 4+ years as of June 1st
  • UKG: Minimum age 5+ years as of June 1st
  • Grade 1: Minimum age 6+ years as of June 1st
  • For higher grades: Transfer certificate and previous academic records required

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Previous Academic Records (report cards)
  • Passport-sized photographs (4 nos.)
  • Parent's ID Proof (Aadhaar/PAN)
  • Residence Proof
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
